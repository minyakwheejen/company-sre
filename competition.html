CATALYST 2023

Collaborative Action to Elaborate Youth Student
About

Collaborative Action to Elaborate Youth Student (Catalyst) adalah suatu rangkaian kegiatan hasil kolaborasi SRE UB yang terdiri dari beberapa rangkaian kegiatan seperti webinar, lomba, campaign, podcast dan exhibition. Catalyst 2023 dipersiapkan mulai dari bulan Desember 2022 hingga dilaksanakan pada bulan Mei 2023. Catalyst 2023 memiliki tema “Accelerate energy transition to renewable earth”.

Vision

Mewujudkan transisi energi, pengembangan wawasan mahasiswa, dan edukasi kepada masyarakat

Mission

Mengembangkan wawasan dan keterampilan mahasiswa, melalui kegiatan edukatif dan lomba memperkenalkan EBT, kepada masyarakat melalui kampanye

Study Case Competition

SCC is a competition for students to design solutions for environmental or renewable energy issues faced by companies. Teams consisting of 3 students from the same university will submit a paper on the first case solution, those who are selected will work on a second case and participate in a pitching day at Brawijaya University as the final stage.

Innovation Paper Competition

IPC is a competition within REVEAL 2024, challenging student teams to present innovations in net zero emission, waste management, green urban design, and intelligent renewable energy. Each team consists 3 students from the same university. The competition starts with abstract submission, followed by a full paper for those who pass, and concludes with poster and PowerPoint creation for the final stage at Universitas Brawijaya.
