Past Activity

SRE UB X DEB Pertamina UB Project

March 2024, SRE UB proudly forged a meaningful partnership with Pertamina to illuminate the path toward sustainable energy solutions. Together, we embarked on a transformative journey to bring solar panel installations to several villages, continue on empowering communities and ushering in a brighter, cleaner future. We’re immensely grateful for the opportunity to work hand in hand with Pertamina and grateful for the chance to make a difference in people’s lives

About This Activity

Desi Energi Berdikari (DEB) is a program launched by the Pertamina Foundation by providing funding to Pertamina Sobat Bumi PF9 & PF10 scholarship recipients from Brawijaya University. Pertamina's DEB program has been implemented since 2019. Since then, the DEB program has produced benefits of 170.880 wp of Solar Power Plant Energy. 605.000 m3/year of biogas and methane gas energy, 8.000 watts of microhydro energy, 6.500 liters of biodiesel energy per year, as well as 16,500 wp of hybrid energy from a solar and wind power plant.
