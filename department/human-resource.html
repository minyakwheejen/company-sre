Human Resource Department

Human resource is a department that is responsible for developing soft skills and good communication between each member of SRE UB through a series of certain activities to add insight and skills in the field of new and renewable energy

Job Description
  • Open SRE recruitment to continue renewable energy-related empowerment
  • Conduct internal activities of SRE's team to boost organization engagement and team development
  • Ensure the great experience of all SRE's team
