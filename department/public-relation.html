Public Relation

The Public Relation department is responsible for building and maintaining relationships between SRE UB and external communities also other institutions

Job Description
  • Building and maintaining good relationships with communities that share mutual goals and directions with SRE UB in a mutually beneficial goals
  • Establishing and maintaining cooperative relationships with both private and government institutions that align with the director of SRE UB, in the form of joint project implementation, project funding, and other related matters
  • Generating and designing campaign ideas resulting from collaborations with other institutions or communities

Past Activity

Company Visit PLTA Bendungan Sutami

On June 2023, SRE UB had the opportunity to visit one of te power plants that supplies electrivity to the island of Java and Bali, namely the Sutami Dam Hydroelectroelectric Power Plant in Malang Regency. We were guided and explained about how the Sutami Dam Hydroelectro electric Power Plant utilize the water level in the dam and we were also invited to see several important points such as water distribution pipes, water turbines and operator rooms.
