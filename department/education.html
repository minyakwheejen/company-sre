Education Departement

Education is the department that is responsible for developing the potential interest and talent of each element of SRE UB in realm education through a series of certain activities to add insights and skills in the field of new and renewable energy.

Job Description
  • Responsible for the education field to facilitate members of SRE UB in expanding knowledge related to renewable energy
  • Arrange and plan the SRE UB's curriculum and activities related on renewable energy education
  • Collaborate with implementation department to pro- vide a research for a new prototype in renewable energy technologies

Past Activity

SRE Review

SRE REVIEW are activities packaged in the form of information publications related to EBT matters. This activity must be carried out by the chairman, secretary and staff of the Education Department in coordination with the Media and Marketing Department. The result of this activity is information packaged in the form of infographics and articles. SRE REVIEW content that has been posted on SRE UB’s instagram containing material about biohydrogen conversion into electricity, unleashing electrical power from Palm Oil Waste (POME), and the lates SRE REVIEW talk about the difference between Indonesia’s and Costa Rica’s regulations related to energy and the environment.

Goals

Meningkatkan wawasan, pengetahuan, dan keterampilan publik tentang EBT melalui publikasi informasi dalam bentuk infografis dan artikel.
