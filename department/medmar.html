Media and Marketing

The Media and Marketing department is responsible for creating, scheduling, and analyzing content such as social media content, documentation, and website content.

Job Description
  • Creating designs related to SRE UB's publication media
  • Conducting market analysis to increase engagement for SRE UB
  • Generating and designing campaign ideas resulting from collaborations with other institutions or communities
  • Maximizing the use of social media to enhance SRE UB's visibility by creating strategic steps that can be implemented by all associates and members
  • Presenting a positive image of SRE UB to external parties
