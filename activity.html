OPEN RECRUITMENT
MEMBER

The Open Recruitment for SRE UB Members is a program aimed at recruiting new members for the SRE UB organization. This activity consists of 3 stages of selection.

  • The collection of documents
    Allows us to review the academic records, experiences, and research interests of the applicants.
  • Leaderless Group Discussion (LGD)
    which provides an opportunity for applicants to discuss topics related to research and technology in groups. We will evaluate collaborative abilities, critical thinking, as well as your ability to articulate ideas and solutions
  • Individual interview with HR
    Here, we will delve deeper into your motivation, your understanding of research and exploration, and how you can actively contribute as a member of SRE UB

FIRST GATHERING
MEMBER

The First Gathering for SRE UB Members is an event aimed at welcoming new members to SRE UB and introducing them to the organization. This event coincides with the first gathering event for REVEAL staff and Study with SRE. The agenda includes an introduction to SRE UB, an explanation of the activities planned, an introduction to REVEAL, and also Study with SRE activities.

TOWNHALL 2
MEMBER

The SRE UB Townhall is an event designed to bring all members of SRE UB together to discuss various aspects related to the organization. This gathering serves as a platform to review and reflect on the first three months of SRE UB activities, including a comprehensive overview of the achievements made, the challenges encountered, and the strategies devised to overcome them. Additionally, the event provides an opportunity to outline SRE UB's plans and goals for the coming months, fostering a collaborative environment for members to share insights and contribute to the organization's future success.

UPGRADING

The Upgrading SRE UB activity with the theme "Social Branding Upgrading" is aimed at enhancing the online presence and professional image of SRE UB members through LinkedIn and Instagram platforms. This activity aims to equip participants with the skills and knowledge needed to effectively utilize these platforms for personal branding and professional networking. This Upgrading session is designed to empower SRE UB members with the necessary tools and strategies to optimize their LinkedIn profiles and leverage other social media platforms for professional growth. Participants will learn how to create an engaging online persona, showcase their skills and experiences, and interact with relevant networks and communities.
