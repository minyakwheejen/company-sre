About us

SRE Brawijaya University

VISION


SRE UB as a renewable-energy-based community creating a renewable energy community which is adaptive, innovative, and contributive

MISSION


  • Enabling Universitas Brawijaya students to collaborate in renewable energy sector projects

  • Having a role as the initiator for Universitas Brawijaya students to actively participate in the renewable energy sector

  • Strongly oriented towards the advancement of new renewable energy

  • Committed to disseminating information with the aim of increasing society's awareness of renewable energy in Indonesia.

  • Actively participate in the innovation and development of new and renewable energy problems in Indonesia
